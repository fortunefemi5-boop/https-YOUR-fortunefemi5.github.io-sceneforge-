Short-form checklist
  • Hook in the first 1–2 seconds — the scene and the first caption should both land immediately.
  • Vertical (9:16) for TikTok, Reels and Shorts; square (1:1) for feed posts; landscape for YouTube.
  • Keep burned-in captions short — four to six words a line reads fastest on a phone.
  • Generate a still in the same scene to use as your cover frame or thumbnail.
  • Match music energy to pacing: drones and lo-fi for slow scenes, chiptune or synthwave for fast cuts.
